[[The pages of the BOOK OF REST AND ENDINGS are filled with obscure bits of cult mumbo-jumbo. However, the following excerpt seems of some interest.]]

THE RITUAL FOR ENDING OF WRATHMEN

From fifty Fathers^
Frozen in slavepast^
Rip from the wraithloom^
Sunder the lifeweave^
Lock tight in earthgrip^
Hold firm in gravefast^
